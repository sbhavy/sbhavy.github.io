I am an MS CS candidate at the University of Southern California (USC), and a Graduate Research Assistant at the [Integrated Media Systems Centre (IMSC)](https://imsc.usc.edu/) at USC, advised by [Prof. Seon Ho Kim](https://sites.google.com/view/skim-home/home). I am broadly interested in Computer Vision and Semi-Supervised Object Detection, particularly in understanding their application to real-world data for object detection and tracking.

Before that, I graduated from the [National Institute of Technology Delhi](https://nitdelhi.ac.in/) as a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering. For my undergraduate thesis, I worked on the detection of violent content in videos using Attention-Augmented Convolutional Networks, under the guidance of [Prof. Anurag Singh](https://nitdelhi.ac.in/profile/Dr.AnuragSingh/index.html).
